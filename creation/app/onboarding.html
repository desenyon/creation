How it works

Prompt, folder, loop.

Enter an optional prompt, choose a local folder, and send. Mission control shows the exact step the agent is running.

  1. 1
    Prompt — describe what to build or leave it blank for market research.
  2. 2
    Folder — choose where the project should live locally.
  3. 3
    Send — Creation researches, Mem0 recalls, SuperCompress compresses, agents build, QA runs, and Composio syncs Linear, GitHub, and Gmail.
